How to Live the Good Life

By: Kimberly Thompson

Love Yourself and Thy Neighbor Live to the Max Stay Healthy Smile

Some helpful hints to living a healthy, loving, holistic life.



1. Love Yourself and Thy Neighbor

Don't listen to the negative comments of others. This will only prevent you from feeling happy about yourself. Keeping this in mind, help out your community as well; it feels good! Examples of this include keeping your community clean, helping an elderly neighbor, or even holding the door for a stranger. Sometimes it's the simple things in life that stand out the most.



2. Live to the Max

Ever wanted to go skydiving? Para sailing? Bungee jumping? Well, then go ahead! Make a list of 25 things you want to do, and gradually, make them happen. Also, whenever a new opportunity arises, never let it slip between your fingers.



3. Stay Healthy

Whenever you exercise, don't you just feel... good? Well, then keep working out on a continual basis; it's a proven fact: those that are healthier are happier. Whether it's a jog around the track, a walk around the block, or a yoga class down the street, be physically active... and avoid junk food!



4. Smile

Whenever you come into contact with people, whether it be in conversation, or a quick walk by, you are constantly judging them. Well guess what, they are also judging you. When approached by a smile, people feel much more at ease as well as relaxed. Greet everyone with a simple "hi." This will give everyone a simple, yet positive first impression of you.



Quick Tips:

  • Sometimes it's okay to be alone; learn to listen to yourself.
  • Most of the time, it's better to love than to hate.
  • With this said, do not become a people pleaser in the process!


Remember: it's okay to make mistakes. Do not blame yourself for them, rather, use them as tools and learn by them.
